About Us

Formed in 2023

End of Tenancy Cleaning London recognizes that arranging end-of-tenancy cleanings can be a daunting task, and choosing the right company can be challenging. We've streamlined this process to alleviate all your stress. Serving London and the rest of the UK, we make your end-of-tenancy cleaning hassle-free.

We understand the importance of a clean and welcoming environment, and our services cater to various needs, including end-of-tenancy cleanings for landlords, preparing homes for new occupants, and assisting individuals during relocation. Our commitment to excellence extends to upcycling usable items, which we donate to charities on your behalf, reducing waste and contributing positively to the community.
